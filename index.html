Academic Excellence

5.5-Year Doctor of Medicine (M.D.) Program

Our flagship 5.5-year Doctor of Medicine (M.D.) Program is designed in accordance with the latest National Medical Commission (NMC) guidelines for Indian students pursuing medical education abroad. The curriculum provides comprehensive medical education through integrated basic sciences, clinical sciences, hospital-based clinical rotations, and a compulsory internship, preparing graduates for global medical practice and eligibility under NMC regulations.

biotech Doctor of Medicine (M.D.)

The Doctor of Medicine (MD) program follows a US-pattern curriculum, widely recognized by international licensing bodies including the Philippines Medical Board, WHO, and the National Medical Commission (NMC) of India. The program features structured pre-clinical and clinical rotation phases.

Program Phases

Years 1–2: Pre-Clinical Sciences Anatomy, Physiology, Biochemistry, Pathology, Pharmacology, and Microbiology with integrated Problem-Based Learning (PBL).
Years 3–4.5: Clinical Clerkship Rotations in affiliated teaching hospitals across Internal Medicine, Surgery, OB-GYN, Pediatrics, and Psychiatry.

Eligibility Criteria

  • 10+2 with PCB (min 50% aggregate, 45% reserved)
  • NEET qualification mandatory for Indian students

Program Highlights

  • NMC & WHO recognized degree
  • Dedicated FMGE / NExT preparation support

Requirement Guidelines

Admission Criteria & Documents

Admission is open to qualified national and international applicants. Please review the checklist below to prepare your enrollment package.

1

Completed Baccalaureate Degree

Must hold a bachelor's degree (pre-med or science course is preferred but not required).

2

NEET Eligibility & Science Qualifications

Indian students must be NEET qualified. All applicants must have obtained at least 50% aggregate marks in Physics, Chemistry, and Biology (PCB).

3

Official Academic Transcripts

Submit certified transcript of records (TOR), diploma copies, and two recommendation letters from undergraduate professors.

4

Personal Interview & Medical Fitness

Applicants undergo an evaluation interview and a physical fitness examination prior to final registration.
